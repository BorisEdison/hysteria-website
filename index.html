EXPERIENCE A TRUE CULTURAL EVENT

As the name suggests Hysteria is a mega festival celebrated annually at the Don Bosco Centre for Learning campus. The main rationale of this fiesta is to fuel the coalescence of the diverse minds and skills our campus is home to. Hysteria provides a platform to all students of various expertise to exhibit their dexterity, competency, virtuosity and enthusiasm in healthy competition to conquer the Hysteria Shield. Housing over 4500 people each year, the power-packed carnival of Don Bosco goes offline this year with exhilarating events ranging from Performing arts to Literary arts, Sports to Gaming and much more...
